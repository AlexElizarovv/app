Ingrid was the main antagonist of the first half of the fourth season of ABC's "Once Upon a Time". She was the aunt of the hero Elsa and is The Snow Queen. She was portrayed by Elizabeth Mitchell.

Personality Edit

Ingrid was a very conflicted woman, filled with bitterness and paranoia, but with the strong desire to have a loving family.

As a child Ingrid loved both her sisters and the three were very close, however after her powers emerged she became fearful, something that was more influenced by being called a monster for her powers.

As an adult, she was fearful and she tried to avoid going out in public because of her lack of control for her powers, much like her niece Elsa. She even considered leaving the kingdom and passing the crown to her sister Helga feeling her powers didn't make her a ruler. However she would use her powers when provoked such as attempting to blast the Duke after repeatedly calling her a monster and a freak that needed to be locked up, something that cost Ingrid her sister Helga.

Ingrid weaped over her sisters body, and hoped to maintain the love of her sister Gerda. SHe was deeply hurt when Gerda rejected her and trapped her in the urn. Upon being trapped Ingrid became bitter and hateful towards "ordinary people", believing that not even friends and family could understand her because of her powers, and that everyone would eventually turn against her and see her as a monster. She also felt a strong dislike for her sister Gerda as a result of her actions, something that passed on to anna for her resemblance. This led her to committing actions in an attempt to drive people with powerful magic such as Emma and Elsa apart from those that she claimed "loved you but fear you". One good example was when she poisons Marian to make people think Elsa did it to turn them against her, and tried to kill hook to achieve the same thing.

However inspite of her bitterness, the one thing she desired most was a loving family. She fell in love with Emma and adoptived her as a child, and was greatly hurt when she ran off. Upon meeting her neice Elsa, she was happy to have a family member that could understand her, however she was less accepting to Anna as she was not only normal but greatly resembled Gerda. This distrust only got worse when Anna went to ask the rock trolls about her and found a hat capable of absorbing magic in her bag. Ingrids newfound hate for Anna was made obvious when she encouraged elsa to freeze her while she was under a spell, attempted to kill her twice.

Even though Anna was under a spell cast by herself when she put Elsa in the urn, Ingrid held Anna in contempt for that. Firmly believng that she was just like her mother, and contributed this to her belief that no one could accept people like her. although Gerda's was voluntary and because of her powers, while Anna was under a spell and was mad at elsa for ignoring her all those years without a single mention of her powers.

Embracing herself as a monster she froze all of Arendelle for 30 years, and planned to cast a spell to kill all the ordinary people. Two reasons for this action is her belief that all ordinary people would turn against them anyways, and to have just herself, Emma and Elsa.

However, after Anna reads a letter from her mother saying that she truly loved ingrid and regretting trapping her away, begging Anna and Elsa to restore everyones memories of her sisters and to free ingrid, Ingrid forgave her sister. After realizing that her sister did love her, she finally realizes what she had done and sacrifices herself to undo the curse. While dieing, she said "you three are so special", showing that she finally saw Anna as part of her family and that she truly accepted herself and Elsa for their powers.
